COMSATS University Islamabad

COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) is a leading public university in Pakistan. Established in 1998, it is committed to promoting education, research, and innovation. The university offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, computer science, and social sciences, and is known for its cutting-edge research and strong focus on technology.
